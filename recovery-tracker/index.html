FAQ

Recovery tracker questions

What is a recovery score?

A recovery (or readiness) score combines overnight heart rate variability, resting heart rate and sleep quality into one number that tells you how ready your body is to train hard today.

Do I need an Apple Watch?

You need a wearable that measures overnight HRV and heart rate and syncs to Apple Health — Apple Watch, Garmin, Whoop, Oura or Fitbit all work. MyVitality reads the data automatically.

How is recovery different from rest?

Rest is taking time off; recovery is the measurable process of adapting and returning to baseline. We break it down further in recovery vs rest.
