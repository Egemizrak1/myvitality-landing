FAQ

Community questions

Is the fitness community public?

You choose. Follow friends and share with them, keep activities private, or post publicly — you control who sees each workout. See our privacy policy.

What are challenges?

Distance or activity goals you can do solo or as a team, with a live leaderboard to keep everyone motivated.

Can I share my workouts to Instagram?

Yes. Every workout becomes a clean route card you can share to Instagram, WhatsApp or anywhere in one tap.
