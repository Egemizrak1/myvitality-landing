About

One app, instead of five

MyVitality is an independent fitness app built around a simple frustration: your training, recovery, sleep and nutrition all affect each other, but they live in apps that never talk.

Our mission

We want to make understanding your body simple. Not more dashboards and more numbers — fewer, clearer signals that actually help you decide what to do today. Train or rest? Push the pace or hold back? Eat more or less? MyVitality brings the data together so the answer is obvious.

Why we built it

Like a lot of people, we were paying for one app to track runs, another for recovery, a third for food, and stitching it together in our heads. The insight was always in the gaps between apps — the way a bad night's sleep shows up in tomorrow's run, or how a hard training block quietly raises your resting heart rate. So we built the app we wanted: everything in one place, on a clean design, with an AI coach that reads all of it.

How we treat your data

Health data is personal, and we treat it that way. Your data is never sold, never shared with advertisers and never used for marketing. It exists to power features inside the app — nothing else. You can read the details in our privacy policy, and you can revoke Health access any time from your iPhone settings.

Independent and focused

MyVitality is built by a small independent team, not a venture-backed data business. That means we answer to the people who use the app, not to advertisers. It also means we move fast — the app is in active beta and shaped directly by early users.
