Everything you need

One app. Full picture.

GPS tracking with dark maps. Apple Watch recovery. AI nutrition. Social sharing. Curated routes. Everything you need — nothing you don't.

🗺️
GPS Workout Tracking
Start a run, ride or hike and watch your route trace itself on a cinematic dark map in real time. Live pace, distance, heart rate, elevation and calorie burn — all on one screen. When you finish, get a full summary with split times and a shareable route card.
💚
Recovery & HRV
Wake up to a daily readiness score calculated from your HRV, resting heart rate and sleep quality. A 14-day HRV trend chart shows your personal baseline so you know when to push and when to rest. All pulled automatically from your Apple Watch — no manual input.
🥗
AI Nutrition
Take a photo of your meal and AI breaks down the calories, protein, carbs and fat in seconds. Search thousands of foods or scan barcodes. Set your own macro goals. The app tells you exactly what you're missing to hit your targets for the day.
😴
Sleep Insights
See your Deep, REM and Light sleep breakdown each morning with a sleep efficiency score. Track how your training load, nutrition and stress levels affect the quality of your sleep over time. Understand your body's recovery patterns, not just the numbers.
⌚
Apple Watch Sync
Every metric your Apple Watch collects — HRV, SpO2, body temperature, VO2 max, respiratory rate, workout calories, sleep stages — automatically synced to MyVitality every 30 minutes. Grant permission once, never think about it again.
🏆
Personal Records & Streaks
MyVitality automatically detects your fastest 1K, 5K, 10K, half and full marathon times. A live activity streak tracks your consistency day by day. Breaking your own records has never felt more satisfying.
👥
Social Feed & Sharing
Follow friends, react to their workouts and share your own with a beautifully designed route card straight to Instagram, WhatsApp or anywhere else. Join groups, post updates and keep each other accountable.
🛣️
Curated Route Maps
Browse a library of running and cycling routes on a stunning dark map. Tap any route to preview the full path, distance, elevation and estimated time — then head out knowing exactly where you're going.

Getting started

Up and running in minutes

No subscriptions. No Whoop band required. Just your iPhone and Apple Watch.

1
Download & create account
Sign up with your email or Google account in seconds. Your data is encrypted and stored securely — never sold to third parties.
2
Connect Apple Health once
Grant HealthKit permission and MyVitality takes over. HRV, sleep stages, heart rate, SpO2, body temperature and more — synced automatically every 30 minutes from your Apple Watch.
3
Pick a route or start from scratch
Browse curated running and cycling routes on the dark map, or just hit Start and go. Your GPS route draws itself in real time with a glowing trail. When you're done, share the route card to Instagram or WhatsApp in one tap.
4
Log food, check recovery, connect with friends
Snap a photo of your meal for instant AI macro breakdown. Wake up each morning to your recovery score and HRV trend. Follow friends, react to their workouts and stay accountable together — all inside one app.
