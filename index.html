Getting started

Up and running in minutes.

No subscriptions. Works with any smartwatch — Apple Watch, Garmin, Polar, Whoop, Oura and more.

1
Download & create account
Sign up with your email or Google account in seconds. Your data is encrypted and stored securely — never sold to third parties.
2
Connect Apple Health once
Grant HealthKit permission and MyVitality takes over. HRV, sleep, heart rate, SpO2 and 20+ metrics — synced automatically every 30 minutes from any device that writes to Apple Health (Apple Watch, Garmin, Polar, Whoop, Oura, Fitbit and more).

Snap a photo of your meal for instant AI macro breakdown. Wake up each morning to your recovery score and HRV trend. Follow friends, react to their workouts and stay accountable together.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

MyVitality is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

  • →Health & Fitness Data — heart rate, HRV, blood oxygen (SpO2), step count, distance, active calories, sleep duration and stages, and workout sessions. Read from Apple Health with your explicit permission.
  • →Location Data — GPS coordinates collected only during an active workout session and only while the app is in use.
  • →Account Data — email address and password (stored as a hashed value) used to authenticate your account.
  • →Usage Data — app interactions and performance logs used to improve the app experience.

2. Health Data — Special Notice

Health and fitness data obtained from Apple Health (HealthKit) is used solely to provide features within MyVitality. This data is:

✓ Never sold to third parties
✓ Never shared with advertisers
✓ Never used for marketing
✓ Accessible only by you

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit and at rest. Passwords are never stored in plain text. Authentication uses short-lived JWT tokens.

4. Third-Party Services

OpenStreetMap — map tiles (no personal data sent).

Google Sign-In — optional auth (governed by Google's Privacy Policy).

5. Your Rights

→ Access the personal data we hold about you
→ Correct inaccurate data or request deletion
→ Revoke Health permissions anytime via iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health
