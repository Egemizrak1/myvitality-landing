FAQ

Sleep tracker questions

How does the sleep tracker work?

MyVitality reads your sleep data from Apple Health, including deep, REM and light stages recorded by your Apple Watch or compatible wearable, and shows a clear breakdown each morning.

How much sleep do I need?

Most adults need seven to nine hours, but consistency and enough deep and REM sleep matter most for performance. See how much sleep you need for better performance.

Can I track sleep without an Apple Watch?

Yes — any wearable or sleep tracker that writes sleep data to Apple Health works, including Oura, Fitbit and Garmin.
