FAQ

Workout tracker questions

Can I track workouts without a smartwatch?

Yes. The workout tracker uses your iPhone's GPS, so you can record runs, rides and walks with just your phone. A smartwatch adds live heart rate and recovery data.

What workout types can I track?

Running, cycling, walking, hiking and more — plus any workout added manually with distance, duration and activity type.

Does it show splits and pace?

Yes. You get live pace, distance, heart rate and elevation while you move, plus per-kilometre or per-mile splits when you finish.
